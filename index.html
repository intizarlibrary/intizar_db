About

Intizarul Imamul Muntazar motsi ne na tarbiyya ga yara ‘yan ƙasa da shekaru 17, ƙarƙashin Mu’assasatu Abul Fadlil Abbas, karkashin jagorancin Sheikh Ibraheem Zakzaky. An kafa shi ne domin gina ruhin yara, tunaninsu da halayensu bisa koyarwar Musulunci. Yana aiki tare da gida, makaranta da al’umma domin shirya su don taimakon Sahibul Asr Waz-Zaman (AJF).

Vision

Samar wa Imam Mahdi (AJF) mataimaka nagartattu.

Mission

Bai wa yara ‘yan ƙasa da shekara 17 tarbiyyar ruhi, jiki da tunani, domin su zama na gari kuma shiryayyu don taimakon Sahibul Asr Waz-Zaman (AJF) karkashin wilaya.

Motto: Inculcating Spiritual and Physical Discipline (Tarbiyyar Ruhi da Gangar Jiki)
